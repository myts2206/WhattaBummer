Lessons to learn from Atomic Habits

Day 1

Goldilocks Rule

It is the act of staying motivated. How exactly do you stay motivated? Imagine a situation where you play tennis. You are asked to play tennis with a four year old. What happens here? You win every point and you get bored after say 5 or 10 shots. Now imagine having to play with a professional tennis player. You play against Serena Williams and hardly win a point. Here too you tend to get demotivated and in a while you quit playing. The trick here is to play with your equals. You win some points and you lose some. You stay motivated and end up playing more matches. Identify your equals and improve step by step.
